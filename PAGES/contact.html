Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the best time to visit Tamil Nadu?

The best time to visit Tamil Nadu is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. Summers can be very hot, especially in the plains.

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour directly through our website by selecting your destination, preferred dates, and accommodations. You can also call us or send an email.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before the tour date. Cancellations within 7 days may incur a fee depending on the booking.

Do you provide airport pickup?

Yes, we provide airport pickup and drop services in Chennai at additional cost. You can add this option during the booking process.

Are meals included in the package?

Our packages include breakfast. Lunch and dinner can be added based on your food preference selection during booking.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable packages. Contact us with your requirements and we'll create a personalized itinerary for you.
